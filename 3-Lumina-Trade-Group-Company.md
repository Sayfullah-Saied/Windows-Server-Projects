## Business Description

Lumina Trade Group is a medium-sized trading company specializing in the import, export, and distribution of consumer and industrial products. The company operates from a single office and warehouse location and requires a secure Windows Server environment to manage users, devices, shared files, and business operations.

## IT Requirements

* Active Directory Domain Services (AD DS)
* DNS and DHCP Services
* File and Print Services
* Group Policy Management
* User Authentication and Access Control
* Data Backup and Recovery
